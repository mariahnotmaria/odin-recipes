Hump Noodles

Description

A family recipe going back at least three generations, Hump Noodles was named by yours truely as a child. Although named after the hump-like shape of macoroni noodles, this recipe can be adapted to any noodle shape you desire.

Ingredients

  • Elbow noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Step One: Brown ground beef.
  2. Step Two: In the same pan, boil elbow noodles for apprx. 8 minutes
  3. Step Three: Add tomato sauce and bring back up to a boil.
  4. Step Four: Add water until desired consistency is achieved. Season and top with spoonful of butter.
