Burritos

Description

A Texas classic, burritos are a staple in many texan's diets. Best enjoyed from a local food truck or taco joint, homemade burritos can be an easy weekday dinner for everybody in your house to enjoy together.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Refied beans
  • Flour tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
  • Yellow onions

Steps

  1. Step One: Chop steak into bulky cubes. Cook until medium rare.
  2. Step Two: Add refried beans to steak. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Step Three: Prepare toppings. Shred cheese. Dice onions.
  4. Step Four: Char tortillas over open flame.
  5. Assemble and serve.
